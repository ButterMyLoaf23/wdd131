Sweet Potato Waffles

Savory waffles made with sweet potato with a hint of ginger.

Prep Time: 30 Min | Cook Time: 30 Min | Servings: 6 waffles

Ingredients:

  • 2 separated eggs
  • 1/4 C Oil
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp Cayenne pepper
  • 3/4 C milk
  • 1 Tbsp Brown Sugar
  • 2 tsp Shredded Ginger
  • 3/4 C Mashed Sweet Potatoes
  • 1 Tbsp Minced Shallots
  • 1 Tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Chives
  • 1/4 C Cornmeal
  • 1 C Flour

Instructions:

  1. Mix egg yolks, oil, salt, cayenne, sugar, ginger, shallots, sweet potatoes, and milk.
  2. Add cornmeal, chives, flour, and baking powder.
  3. Whip egg whites until stiff and fold in.
  4. Cook until golden brown in a waffle iron. Serve with butter and wilted greens or honey.
